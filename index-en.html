Globally Consistent Quality

Mastek Kauçuk is a part of the global supply chain. Thanks to its international trade experience, Mastek exports its goods to all around the world. Apart from its operations in Türkiye, Mastek products has been and still used in factories in countries such as: Brazil, Germany, Iran, Romania, Russia, South Africa and Spain. The international presence of Mastek is mostly in the automotive industry and Mastek is looking forward to expand its presence by producing for other industries as well.

Vision

Through targeting highest level of satisfaction of our customers, we will be a company that continuously increases customer satisfaction.

Mission

In terms of quality, price and delivery time and in the context of new products, the customer expectations are going to be satisfied by respecting both the nature and our workforce, while simultaneously improving and upgrading our workforce and processes, to ultimately increase the ‘Company Value’.

Quality Policy

Our Quality Policy consists of: being a trusted supplier and a business partner for our customers, increasing our customer and labourer satisfaction, improving our processes continuously and finding resources to suffice the demands for this and finally applying all of the possible conditions for our customers.

Occupational Health, Safety and Environment

Our firm aims to raise awareness in the context of environment, occupational health and safety. In order to achieve this, we put preserving the environment, occupational health and safety as a core principle and apply the following principles:

  • By using the natural resources, energy, raw materials and equipment efficiently, we strive to decrease pollution as much as possible.

  • Minimizing the usage of hazardous chemicals and to eliminate them completely if possible. Taking precautions in every step to continuously have zero environmentally detrimental accidents

  • Decreasing and pacifying waste resources to have a smaller carbon footprint.

  • Supporting reuse and recycling as much as possible.

  • Taking precautions in every step to have zero accidents and to protect our workforce from health deteriorating conditions.

  • Raising awareness in terms of environment, occupational health and safety of our workforce.

  • Complying with the regulations regarding environment and occupational safety.

AUTOMOTIVE

Sealants, acoustic pads and structural reinforcement parts

Mastek produces products for the automotive industry in three main types. Die cut, roll and tape. You can check our products below accordingly to their types. At the same time, Mastek uses various coating options and their combinations to make the parts ready for usage. These coating options are such as but not limited to: nonadhesive paper, aluminium, fiber and various films.

Tape

Our tape products in general, after being heated in their respective conditions expand, stick or stiffen. Especially in the automotive sector, Mastek is part of the supply chain of major companies due to its delivery of agile and creative solutions while not making any compromises in terms of quality.

Rolled Tape

Accordingly to the needs, our products can be rolled with non-adhesive release papers. These products are often used by our customers who desire variable tape lenghts rather than a fixed sized product.

Die Cut Parts

We use a die cutting for parts that are going to be assembled in a very precise location of the car. We design our parts to make them fit perfectly in the designated spots identified by our customers.

White Goods

Our products can be formed accordingly to the project specifications...

Since the year 2000, Mastek's accumulated knowledge and expertise in the white goods industry has been substantiated through various parts, as well as tape and rolled tape products. Today, Mastek utilizes its experince and know-how to offer competitively priced high quality parts for the white goods sector.

Tape and Rolled Tape

One of the major problems to be resolved in the white goods industry is isolation. Isolation needs could be related to sound, vibration or heat. Mastek answers all of these needs with long lasting high quality solutions that it offers.

Construction

Our products can be formulized chemically to answer your expectations.

With its high quality standard, Mastek produces parts in a wide array for the construction industry as well. From window assembly, to various box joints and even roof isolation, Mastek is a trusted partner of the construction industry.

Rolled Tape

Rolled tape products are ideal for variable lenght requirements. Construction sector has many changing variables leading to unpredictable application area and lenght. Hence, Mastek's trusted rolled tape is the solution to the sector's problem.
